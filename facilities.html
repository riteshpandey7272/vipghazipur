Infrastructure & Facilities

  • Home
  • Infrastructure & Facilities

Classrooms & Tutorial rooms

 

The institutes has spacious classrooms with comfortable seating arrangement in the academic block. The classrooms and tutorial rooms are equipped with AV facilities like OHP, VCR and LCD/DLP projectors.

 

Laboratories

 

The degree course in pharmacy requires laboratory work to compliment theory lectures. The institute has state of the art laboratories for all the disciplines of pharmacy. They are specially designed to provide optimum space, light and working ease to each student.

  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry 1
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry 2
  • Unit Operations
  • Pharmacology
  • Pharmacognoscy
  • Human Anatomy Physiology and Patho-physiology
  • Microbiology
  • Pharmaceutics
  • Advanced Pharmocology
  • Pharma-technology (Pharmaceutics)

 

 

Library

 

Besides departmental libraries, the Institute has a central library with book bank facility. The library assets are getting enriched on a regular basis. There are a wide range of books, periodicals, journals, theses, microfilms and compact discs available in the library. 
To avail library facilities a student has to abide by the rules of the institute.

 

 

 

Computer Centre

 

The institute has a 1600 sq ft, fully air-conditioned, well-equipped, cutting edge, multi-lab Computer Center consisting:

  • Core 2 Duo workstations in the ratio of 1:1.
  • High end IBM Server with Windows.
  • LAN connectivity through structured cabling.
  • Round the clock Internet Connectivity through Broadband connection of upto 2 Mbps.
  • Wireless fidelity (wi-fi) building, enabling students to use internet from all parts of the institute.
  • Peripheral support including Scanners, Laser Printers, Color Inkjets, Dot Matrix Printers, CD writers etc.
  • 100% power backup with a stand-by Generator.
  • Exemplary maintenance schedule and upgradation.

 

Hostel Facility

 

Residing in hostel provides students with the opportunity to experience living in a community dedicated to the academic and personal development of individual members. Hostel accommodation is available to all the students. We have separate hostel facilities for girls and boys. To provide an environment conducive to academic and personal development, a code of conduct & norms are enforced for all residents of hostel.

 

Pharmaceutical Society

 

The students of HI-PER organise seminars festivals, workshops, cultural activities etc. under the aegis of Pharmaceutical Society. New students also get a chance to integrate with existing students to make a healthy and creative ambience in the campus.

 

Sports Facilities

 

Institute has a sports department which organises sports activities with in the institute and without. Outdoor games like Volyball, Cricket, Badminton etc are complimented by indoor games like Carrom, Chess, Table Tennis etc. The students of HI-PER organise seminars festivals, workshops, cultural activities etc. under the aegis of Pharmaceutical Society. New students also get a chance to integrate with existing students to make a healthy and creative ambience in the campus.

 

Transportation

 

The institute bus is available as conveyance covering all the major routes in Ghazipur.
